HIBE-Lab

Healthy and Intelligent Built Environment-Lab

HIBE-Lab is dedicated to advancing human-centered research on intelligent, resilient, and sustainable buildings, spanning from micro to macro scales. The primary focus lies in intelligent control, human-built environment interaction, and building performance optimization. Current research topics include: Indoor personal thermal comfort and control; Human-built environment interactions;
Digital twin in the built environment; Workplace safety and health.
